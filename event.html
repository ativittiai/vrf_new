Artificial Intelligence (AI) workshop

venue : RIMT Universty, Mandi (Punjab)
date : 05-02-2020

Vitti Research Foundation successfully conducted Artificial Intelligence (AI) workshop at RIMT Universty, Mandi Punjab. Mr. Kundana Kumar Lal, President of Vitti Research Foundation sensitised the students of RMIT university on AI. He talked about the penetration of AI in our day to day life, its impact and the rise of AI. The potential risk AI has over jobs and industry automation and its domain specific importance. In all, the session was an eye –opener for the students, faculties and the management to think about the futuristic technology and its prospects for their students in today’s market.

AI in Design workshop

venue : World University of Design (WUD), Sonipat
date : 03-02-2020

Vitti Research Foundation (VRF) launched 5 days/40 hours of AI in Design workshop at the World University of Design (WUD). Inaugurated by Dr. Sanjay Gupta, Vice-Chancellor WUD. First, of its kind, this unique initiative by VRF threw light on various aspects of AI and how it can solve industry-specific problems. Further, it sensitized the budding youth on leveraging AI technologies in augmenting Design processes.
